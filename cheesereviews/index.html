Cheese Reviews

Hello, welcome to Jaden Walker's cheese reviews! Jaden has made it his lifes mission to finding the best cheeses and a ranking structure to judge them. Now he's ready to share them with you!

Ranking Structure

  • Appearance (/5): If something doesn't look edible, why would anyone want to eat it? Cheese comes in all different shapes and sizes, colors and presentations. As such, appearance will constitute towards rank.
  • Texture (/10): Many people hate onions because of how slimey they taste. Jaden hates onions. Fuck onions. If any cheese has a texture like an onion, it will get a 0 in this category.
  • Smell (/5): You know that smell a dishwasher inevitably gets where it smells like dead fish, but you never even put fish in it? If any cheese tastes like dead fish, I swear to god I'll puke and then give it a 0 in this category.
  • Taste (/15): Some people say something is "cheesey", but they mean that as a term of endearment. This category literally judges how much cheese tastes like cheese, you'd think it'd be hard to fuck this one up but you'll be surprised.

Top Reviews

  • Anthony's Pizza (Pizza, mozzarella) (8/35):
    Appearance (1/5): Maybe they didn't cook the pizza long enough, but the cheese isn't even melted. Like who even does that? If I wanted unmelted cheese I'd go scoop some out of a bag from the grocery store that has enough preservatives for years.
    Texture (3/10): This would be a 1 if not for the few parts of the edges that were actually melted.
    Smell (1/5): It smelt like unmelted mozzarella, so nothing really special.
    Taste (3/15): Pretty below average, mostly due to not being melted. Nothing to really write home about.
  • Burg's Burger Emporium (Burger, cheddar) (33/35):
    Appearance (8/5): Finally, cheese that is properly melted and even has a nice light sear around the edges but no air bubbles.
    Texture (9/10): The texture of cheddar atop a burger without being too melted or not melted enough is always good to see.
    Smell (4/5): The smell complimented the burger well but was not overpowering.
    Taste (12/15): Quality slab of cheese is always a nice snack. Tasted fresh without being *too* fresh.
